MeisterMade Crafts

My wife is exceptionally talented – she's a crafter! In 2016 she created an Etsy shop to share her projects, and we thought it would be great for her to have a professional web presence representing her business. In 2018 I decided to revamp her website by learning Ruby on Rails.

There are three main components to this site. First, a public-facing showcase of the business, its products and customer feedback. Second, an administrative dashboard that makes it easy to manage customer reviews, products and categories. And third, a platform to share blog posts in order to promote the business and document my wife's journey as a small business owner. I did my research and learned how to build the models, controllers and views to represent reviews, categories, products and blog posts. I also implemented a secure user system (with Devise) to allow my wife to easily manage the site and post to the blog.


Luxemburg-Casco Academic & Career Planning

In 2016 Luxemburg-Casco School District put together a committee to create a new Academic & Career Planning program for its students. This program is tasked with educating and aiding students about their future after high school, whether that means joining the workforce or pursuing higher education. The group wanted to build an interactive web presence that could help inform families.

After consulting with the team about design and functionality, I set to work building a simple, responsive site that would convey information in a friendly format for students and parents. I learned how to manipulate SVG objects (using Snap and Javascript) to create interactive elements, and I built the site on Bootstrap with mobile in mind.
